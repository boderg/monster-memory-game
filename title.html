How to Play


  1. Click Enter to go to the game page
  2. Click any card to start the game
  3. Match cards by clicking on two cards in turn
  4. If the cards match they remain turned
  5. If the cards do not match they flip back (try again)
  6. The game ends when all cards are matched
  7. The Moves counter records how many moves you have taken (1 move per pair of cards turned)
  8. The timer records how long it has taken to complete all matches
  9. The reset button resets the game and randomises the cards
  10. The cards are reset and randomised at the end of every game
